Global Control Baseline

Lift is normalized against "All pages NOT receiving a link" to filter out site-wide seasonality and Google updates.

functions
Bayesian Smoothing

Uses hierarchical priors to avoid noise on low-traffic pages, delivering reliable "Probability of Uplift" scores.

GSC Latency

Google Search Console data typically arrives with a 48-72 hour lag. Performance is refreshed daily.
